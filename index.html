About MindWell

Our Mission

MindWell is dedicated to providing accessible mental health screening tools to help people understand their mental wellbeing. Our assessment is based on established psychological principles and designed to provide meaningful insights.

While our tool is not a substitute for professional diagnosis, it can help you identify areas of concern and provide guidance on when to seek professional help.

How It Works

Our assessment consists of 48 questions that evaluate three key dimensions of mental health:

  • Anxiety - Measures feelings of nervousness, worry, and panic
  • Depression - Assesses mood, interest in activities, and energy levels
  • Stress - Evaluates coping abilities and pressure perception

After completing the assessment, you'll receive a detailed breakdown of your results along with personalized suggestions for improving your mental wellbeing.

Our Team

MindWell was developed by a team of mental health professionals, psychologists, and software developers committed to making mental health resources more accessible to everyone.

We regularly update our assessment based on the latest research and user feedback to ensure we're providing the most accurate and helpful information possible.
